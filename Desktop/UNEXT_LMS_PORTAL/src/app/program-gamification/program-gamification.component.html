Course Weightage Logic Instructions

Formula = (C1*W1) + (C2*W2) + (C3*W3) + (C4*W4)
  • Admin can assign %age weightage to each course based on which total points for a Term would be calculated.
  • Let us suppose MBA Jan 21 Sem 1 has four courses and each of then have equal weightages, points for learner for each of these courses are as follows; Course 1 = 100 Points, Course 2 = 150 Points, Course 3 = 200 Points, Course 4 = 250 Points
    • Total points at Term level = Course 1 points * weightage of Course 1 + Course 2 points * weightage of Course 2 + Course 3 points * weightage of Course 3 + Course 4 points * weightage of Course 4
    • Total points at Term level = 100 * 0.25 + 150 * 0.25 +200 * 0.25 + 250 * 0.25 = 175
! Please make sure, the total sum of Percentages is 100
